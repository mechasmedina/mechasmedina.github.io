About Me

I'm Mechas Medina — a bilingual project manager with 19+ years running digital and creative projects across Latin America and globally. I've worked inside agencies, startups, and media companies, always in the space where ideas meet execution.

My background spans advertising production, digital platforms, e-commerce, content, and Web3. I'm Scrum-certified and I integrate AI tools into my daily workflow to move faster and document smarter.

I'm based in Mexico City and work with teams remotely across time zones. I care about clarity, momentum, and making sure the work actually ships.

Website Luxe Source

Project Type

Websites

Link

Luxe Magazine

Full-cycle UX/UI design and development engagement for Luxe Source, a premium luxury lifestyle platform.

Scope covered the design and build of new interactive modules including a lead generation quiz with HubSpot integration, first-time user experience (FTUE) with animated onboarding, tooltips, a Gold List page, events page, single-image URLs, and a paid "Premium" results ticker.

Development work included a brand refresh rollout with updated typography and logos, SEO-focused content archiving, tag cleanup, regional content filtering, and a video repository landing page. Full QA/UAT with nightly builds, site launch, analytics implementation, and ongoing program management with bi-weekly sprint planning — delivered in close collaboration with the Luxe internal team.

UX/UI: Coco

Website – AFN

Project Type

Websites

End-to-end brand creation, strategic communications, and digital development for the Amazonia Finance Network, an IDB Invest initiative mobilizing sustainable finance across the Amazon basin.

Scope included the full visual identity system (logo, color palette, typography, and brand guidelines), trilingual content production in English, Spanish, and Portuguese across narrative, marketing, and event materials — including a content library, three-fold brochure, social media assets, and HD video production with voice-over and subtitles in all three languages.
